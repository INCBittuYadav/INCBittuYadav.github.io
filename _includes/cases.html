Increase internal transparency of valuable data

Many organisations also fail to break down internal data silos, impeding data sharing and collaboration across an organisations business units. According to a study among 1000 business leaders and technology managers it was discovered that

  • 45 percent say that data sharing can not happen freely in their organisation.
  • 91% of the surveys IT respondents say data is split between multiple cloud systems, a mix of cloud and on-premises systems, or only on-premises systems, which impacts ubiquity of access.

Instead of exposing raw data to wide range of internal data consumers which are more and more non-technical business people, data is productized into easy to consume valuable data products. The productized layer of data is the key in increasing the internal transparency of valuable data.

Solution provided by ODPS 2.0

ODPS is suitable protocol to break down the silos and internal organizational borders between business units. With help of ODPS data sharing can be enabled with one clear metadata model.

Standard business metadata elements to express what is the purpose and value proposition of the data.

  • ODPS 2.0 includes clear business level model with more than 120 attributes
  • contains use case example description to provide more information how to generate value from the data.

Machine-readable clearly defined access to data.

  • ODPS 2.0 contains standardized model to define data access with 9 attributes.

Data democratization is built on top of transparency. That is enabled by decoupling data from systems in order to provide easier and faster data discovery and comparison.
